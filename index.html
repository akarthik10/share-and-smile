How this works?

Share something and bring a Smile!   Sign-in to get started and start bringing smiles.

Have you ever wondered how your junk can help someone in need of it? Say, you have a bunch of useless gadgets or books. Instead of throwing it in trash, you could give it to someone who is in need of it. If you run a hotel, you can give your left-over food to the nearest orphanage instead of wasting it. The result? Smiles in many faces.

About

Share and Smile is a platform for users to share things. It allows users to post "events" or give-aways which other users can utilize. Not just things, you also share a smile.

How can I contribute?

You have to login using your Google account, and on the first time you will be asked to provide your location. Once your location is registered, you can see all the events that other people near your place have created. An "event" is an instance where a person is ready to give away something. You can also provide an optional video describing your event while creating it. Interested people will contact you, and you can share your stuff and have a smile.


How can I accept a give-away?

You can search for a particular item either using the map by selecting nearest markers, or you can filter events based on their category, like food, books, electronics and so on. After you select a category, only the events belonging to that category are shown on the map and the list.


What if I don't have access to internet?

Share and Smile also supports SMS interface which you can use to interact with this application. Once you link your mobile number with your account, you can get customized messages according to your profile settings.

What's the "Home Page"?

The page which is shown immediately after you have logged in successfully is the Home Page. It has a Category List on the left side, Map with event markers in the middle and Event List on the right side.

How does the Category List on the left side works?

The category list acts as a filter and shows only events of the selected category on the map and on the event list

What is an Event?

An event is an instance where a user is ready to give-away something. If you have some old books and you want to share it, you have to create an event first.

What after the event is completed successfully?

You can manage your events by selecting the My Events link from your profile. You can delete the events once they have been completed.

What is the Event List on the right side?

The Event List lists the selected category events in the nearest-first order. Once you move your mouse over any list item, a quick snap of it is shown on the map.

How do I view the details of an event?

To view the details of an event, click on the map marker for that event, or select an event from the event list.

How can I contact the event creator?

The phone number of the event creator is shown in the call button if provided. Other means of contact include the email option or the Hangout option from their Google+ profile.

How can I reach the event location?

Use the directions panel on the right side of the event detail page which lists Public transport, driving or walking instructions to the place of interest.

I need some clarifications about the event from the event creator

Please use the comments section to discuss all the event related activities

The map shows a circled number instead of marker

To prevent the overcrowding of markers on the map, markers are clustered. Clicking on any cluster will reveal other markers within that cluster

How can I change my location?

Please use the Settings link from the profile dropdown to select the option change my location

Use the settings link on the top right profile menu, and select the Add Phone button. This displays a registration code.

Use this to register your mobile number. Ex: If your code is abcde then send an SMS @shareandsmile.register abcde to the above said number. You will get an Authentication Successful message on success

Send an SMS @shareandsmile to get your nearest events. Your location information is taken automatically from your profile and the nearest five events are returned.

Use @shareandsmile.category Ex: @shareandsmile.books to retrieve a list of only events of that category.
